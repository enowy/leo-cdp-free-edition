LEO CDP is the Customer Data Platform for on-premises deployments, is developed by USPA Technology Company.
There are 5 main functions of LEO CDP
  1. Provide a customer analytics platform for data-driven organizations
  2. Data Unification: Consistent & Unified 360 degree Customer View
  3. Provide a visual data segmentation tool for marketing team 
  4. Leveraging AI, Machine Learning and Big Data for Smart Personalization
  5. Provide predictive marketing automation technology to activate your data-driven business
